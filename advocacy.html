Advocacy Work

I am an advocate for underserved student populations and I work to expand access to the kinds of educational opportunities that changed my own life. My efforts have garnered local, state, and national attention.

Selected Press & Media

The New York Times
I was recognized in the NYT for my work in opposition to legacy preference in college admissions.
AP News
I shared my reflections on how college access policy often overlooks rural and first-generation students, and how those gaps shape outcomes.
CT Public
I spoke before the CT legislature to explain why ending legacy admissions matters for students who have never benefitted from inherited privilege.
CT Post
I discussed how reform efforts collide with institutional resistance and what that means for equity.
Yale Daily News — Profile
I reflected on my path as an FGLI advocate and how my rural upbringing shaped my work.
The Hill
My story was used to highlight the structural barriers facing rural students and why policymakers must take them seriously.
Yale Daily News
"Yale's Dean of Admissions and I offered competing testimonies at the Connecticut State Legislature."
Yale Daily News — Feature
